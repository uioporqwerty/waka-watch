Description

Waka Watch allows you to view your WakaTime stats right on your Apple Watch.

Features:

  • View your daily coding activity on your Apple Watch face.
  • Get notifications on your Apple Watch when you've completed a WakaTime goal.
  • See your coding summary over 5 days and your position on the public leaderboard.
