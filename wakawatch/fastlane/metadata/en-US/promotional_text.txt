Waka Watch allows you to know about your coding habits right from your Apple Watch. With Waka Watch, you'll be able to do the following:

- Get notified when you've achieved a goal.
- View your current coding activity for the day on your watch face.
- Find your standing on the public leaderboard and view the standing of others.
- See graphs about your coding activity over the last 5 days.
- View your WakaTime profile and the profile of others.
